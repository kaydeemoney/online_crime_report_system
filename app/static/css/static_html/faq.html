Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is the Public Service Online Crime Reporting System?

The Public Service Online Crime Reporting System is an initiative to facilitate the reporting of cybercrime, fraud, and other online offenses. It provides a secure platform for residents and visitors of Nigeria to report incidents to law enforcement agencies.

Who can use this system?

This system is available to anyone residing in or visiting Nigeria who wishes to report incidents of online crime. Both victims and witnesses of such offenses are encouraged to utilize this platform.

Is my report confidential?

Yes, your privacy is highly prioritized. All information submitted through the system is treated with utmost confidentiality and in compliance with data protection regulations.

Reporting Process

How do I report an online crime?

To report an online crime, navigate to the 'Report' section on our website and fill out the incident report form. Provide as much detail as possible, including date, time, location, and description of the incident.

Can I report anonymously?

Yes, you have the option to submit your report anonymously if you prefer not to disclose your identity. However, providing contact information can help authorities follow up effectively.

What happens after I submit a report?

Once you submit a report, our team reviews the information and forwards it to the appropriate law enforcement agency for investigation. You may be contacted for additional details if necessary.

Technical Support

Who can I contact for technical support?

If you encounter technical issues while using the system, please contact our technical support team at techsupport@ekiticivilservice.gov.ng or call +234-XXX-XXXX.

How secure is the online reporting system?

The system employs state-of-the-art security measures to protect your data. All communications are encrypted, and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
