What We Do

The Public Service Online Crime Reporting System serves as a vital link between the community and law enforcement agencies. Our primary objective is to provide a streamlined and accessible channel for reporting various types of online crimes, ensuring prompt intervention and investigation by relevant authorities.

Through this platform, individuals can report incidents such as:

  • Cybercrime: Including phishing scams, hacking attempts, identity theft, and online fraud.
  • Fraudulent Activities: Reports on financial scams, deceptive practices, and fraudulent schemes.
  • Other Online Offenses: Any suspicious or criminal activities encountered online that threaten personal or public safety.

How It Works

Reporting an incident through our system is straightforward and confidential. Users can submit detailed information about the incident, including date, time, location (if applicable), and a comprehensive description of what occurred. This information is securely transmitted to our dedicated team, who ensure that each report is handled with the utmost care and urgency.

Known and Unknown Reporting

We encourage both known and unknown reporters to use our platform. Whether you are a victim directly affected by an incident or a concerned citizen who has witnessed suspicious activities, your report plays a crucial role in safeguarding the community. Every report is carefully reviewed, and appropriate actions are taken to address and mitigate the reported issues.

Why Report?

Reporting online crimes is not only a civic responsibility but also a proactive measure to protect yourself and others from potential harm. By reporting suspicious activities promptly, you contribute to the prevention of future incidents and help law enforcement agencies in their efforts to combat cyber threats effectively.

Privacy and Security

We prioritize your privacy and ensure that all information submitted through our system is handled securely and in accordance with applicable data protection laws. Confidentiality is maintained throughout the reporting process, and personal details are protected to the highest standards.

Get Involved

The success of our system relies on community involvement. We encourage you to explore our resources, learn about common online threats, and stay informed about best practices for online safety. Together, we can create a safer digital environment for all residents and visitors of Nigeria.

Contact Us

For more information about the Public Service Online Crime Reporting System or to report an incident, please contact us through our dedicated channels. Your feedback and contributions are invaluable in our ongoing efforts to enhance community safety and security.

Thank you for your commitment to making Nigeria a safer place to live, work, and thrive.
