Our Services

Make a Report

Users of our online crime reporting system have the option to make reports either as a known user or anonymously. This flexibility ensures that individuals can choose the level of confidentiality they prefer when submitting information about cybercrime, fraud, or any online offense.

Follow Up on Reports

After submitting a report, users can track the progress of their case through our system. We provide updates and notifications to keep you informed about the status of investigations and any actions taken by law enforcement authorities.

Access to Resources

Our platform offers access to educational resources and information about online safety. We aim to empower users with knowledge to protect themselves against cyber threats and to promote digital literacy within our community.
