About Us

Meet Kingsley

Kingsley is a dedicated final year student at Nigeria University, pursuing a degree in Software Engineering. With a passion for technology and innovation, he is committed to leveraging his skills to contribute positively to society.

Project Overview

As part of his final year project, Kingsley is developing the Public Service Online Crime Reporting System. This system aims to provide a secure and efficient platform for residents and visitors of Nigeria to report incidents of cybercrime, fraud, and other online offenses.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance community safety and security by empowering individuals to report online crimes promptly and effectively. We strive to create a safer digital environment through proactive measures and collaboration with law enforcement agencies.

Get Involved

We encourage active participation from the community in utilizing our platform. Your support and feedback are invaluable as we continue to develop and improve the online crime reporting system.
